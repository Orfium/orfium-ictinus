## Overview

Border radius tokens define the curvature of the corners of an element's border. These tokens provide a consistent and modular way of defining the border radius for various components such as buttons, cards, and input fields.

## Tokens

Border Radius is part of [globals](../?path=/docs/design-tokens-about--overview#globals) tier of tokens.

<RadiusShowcase />

Tokens are represented in `px` for the convenience of Figma.
